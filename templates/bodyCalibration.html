BODY CALIBRATION

Starting Point

Depending on your gender, age and height there are optimal body fat percentages that we recommend you should be in to maintain a healthy lifestyle. For males we recommend sub 14% as a good starting point and for women sub 23%. At the initial consultation, after taking your reading we find out what your optimal percent will be then gear the training and nutrition towards that.

Body Fat Readings

Body calibration is one of the most effective ways of measuring progress. Using callipers, specific sites around the body are measured and once combined with hight and weight are put into an algorithm to calculate body fat, muscle mass and other readings that can be documented. Based on these readings, our team can also get a good understanding of hormone production in the body. When trying to get into shape hormones play a essential role in maximising results.

Chart Tracked

Once we have all your data we place it onto a graph so we can visually track your progress along your journey. If for any reason we start to see your progress plateau we can quickly manipulate your training or diet to counter act this and keep you achieving and performing at your best.
