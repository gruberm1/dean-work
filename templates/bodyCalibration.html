BODY CALIBRATION

Depending on your gender, age and height there are optimal body fat percentages that we recommend you should be in to maintain a healthy lifestyle. At the initial consultation we find out what your optimal percent will be then gear the training and nutrition towards that. Once we have all your data we place it onto a graph so we can visually track your progress along your journey. If for any reason we start to see your progress plateau we can quickly manipulate your training or diet to counter act this and keep you achieving and performing at your best.
